OUR BLOGGER - MABEL

Meet Mabel our local music enthusiast with a passion for collecting vinyl and CDs. Her heart beats to the rhythm of timeless tunes as she enjoys the melodies of many bygone eras. In a world where digital is the standard Mabel finds solace in the crackling sound of a needle dropping on vinyl, and the nostalgia that she gets from her CDs spinning in her vintage CD player.

Mabel’s heart belongs to the many boybands of the early 90s to the early 2010s, from the iconic hits of The Beatles to the synchronized moves of The Backstreet Boys she’s made it her mission to collect every artist's work.

When Mabel isn’t lost in her collection of music she’s likely at her favourite record store hunting vinyl and CDs, or attempting to tune the Ukuleles at Flying Tiger. Her room is a treasure trove of musical history, where each piece tells a story and carries the echoes of a different time.

Her musical knowledge is unparalleled, so if you wish to discuss the evolution of boybands or discover any hidden gems in her collection please feel free to message her on her social media.

“I wish today was as good as the songs of yesterday” – Our Best Girl Mabel
