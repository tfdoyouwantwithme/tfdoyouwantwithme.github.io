How should you store your vinyl?

06/11/2023


PLASTIC ALBUM COVERS

WHERE TO STORE THEM

Part of this article might sound stupid, but it is especially important to store your vinyl records as your top priority.

The Plastic Album Cover:

Most albums already come in a plastic cover, especially second-hand ones. The plastic cover is to prevent breakage of the cardboard and to prevent dust from ruining the vinyl itself. Make sure you buy these plastic covers when you buy new vinyl records, so they stay new! You can buy them in packs online or your local record store. You will find them in sizes 12” and 7”.

Where to store them:

If you have a small collection and not a professional rare vinyl collection, always find a shelf that is specifically for vinyl records. Do not put anything heavy on the same shelf so you prevent any shelves falling and damaging your vinyl records. If there are two shelves, always put records on the top shelf. Having vinyl records on the bottom shelf, you could risk damage from shelves falling or dust. Also, always store your vinyl records vertically as it helps prevent warping.


This is personal opinion. It is important to keep vinyl records safe and far from damaged. Hope this helps!
