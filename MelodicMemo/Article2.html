Are vinyl records worth it?

06/11/2023

Are they worth it when Spotify and iTunes exist? Some people buy vinyl records for the aesthetic and others to have a professional rare albums collection. But why? Can’t they find them as audios online?


The feeling of having a physical vinyl that you can play on an old school record player is magical somehow. When starting a collection, at first it was just collecting your favourites but in someway it turns into an obsession to buy full collections just to say you have them (let’s be honest … we all do this). It is important to try latest music and vinyl records help you with that.

When buying a vinyl record that has one song you know, there is a chance you’ll here the rest of the album and enjoy it. On streaming platforms, you listen to that one song and don’t open up to the music the rest of the album has to offer. Really the whole purpose of vinyl records now a days is to have collections. But it is still magical owning a hard copy of a song. Can you believe a song plays out of a plastic disk with lines grooved in it?


Overall, vinyl records are for the responsible and are definitely worth it especially to try new genres of music and to connect with the full albums and not just the songs you like. Plus, they sometimes have hidden sings etched on vinyl records.
